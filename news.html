Energya awarded ISO 45001:2018 Certificate.Tower is Fabricated by Energya

Our company was established in 2006 to serve the requirements of a wide range of industrial projects, Our production capacity is approximately 150,000 Metric Tons per year. The fabrication capacity of tonnage for the individual line of activity is outlined hereunder: Structure Steel Fabrication (heavy, medium & light) with capacity of 24,000 MT/annum. Plate Works of process steel equipment with capacity of 12,000 MT/annum. Hot dip galvanization plants with capacity of 100,000 MT/ annum. Transmission and Telecommunications Towers with capacity of 12,000 MT/annum. Pre-engineered Building with capacity of 6,000 MT/annum. Fabrication of Steel spare parts of Cement plants, Steel mills, Petrochemicals Plants and Power Stations.
